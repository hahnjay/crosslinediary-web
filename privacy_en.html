Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 19, 2026

1. Personal Information Collected

Dear. Nosy (hereinafter the 'App') collects the following minimum personal information for membership registration and service provision.

  • Required items: Email address, nickname, unique device identifier (linked information for social login)
  • Optional items: Profile image

2. Purpose of Collection and Use of Personal Information

The App utilizes the collected personal information for the following purposes.

  • Fulfillment of contracts regarding service provision and provision of customized services (e.g., generation of AI interference replies)
  • Member management: Identity verification according to the use of membership services, personal identification, prevention of unauthorized use by bad members

3. Period of Retention and Use of Personal Information

In principle, after the purpose of collecting and using personal information has been achieved, the relevant information is destroyed without delay. However, if it is necessary to preserve it in accordance with the provisions of relevant laws and regulations, it is preserved for a certain period.

4. Diary Data Processing (E2EE)

The user's diary data is stored on the device with End-to-End Encryption (E2EE), and neither the developer nor any third party can read the contents of the diary. Data transmitted for AI analysis is destroyed immediately after processing.
